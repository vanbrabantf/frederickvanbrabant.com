# AI usage on this website

I occasionally use AI tools for research purposes, and any research generated by these tools is carefully vetted and verified. All the text that is written on this blog is from my own mind, and I like to keep it that way; it is Frederick Vanbrabant's delirous rantings after all.

In the earlier days I did some experimentation with AI grammar and spelling corrections, it sometimes reworded parts of the text. Not something I liked but I mainly used it because I was uncertain of my skills as a writer. These days I refrain from doing that, even if that means more clumsy articles.

The header images used to be AI generated. It always felt bad to me so I replaced them all with self-made pictures. I did study photography at some point so this is a nice way to showcase my pictures. Most of them are made in Antwerp, others from various trips I've done.

The very old ones (2020) are stock images
